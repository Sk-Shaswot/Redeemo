How to Earn Points on Reedemo

Reedemo offers multiple ways to earn points that you can redeem for exciting rewards. Here's how you can maximize your earnings:

Ways to Earn

  • Complete Quests: Participate in daily or weekly quests like logging in consecutively, finishing tasks, or submitting feedback.
  • Invite Friends: Get bonus points for every friend you refer who joins the platform.
  • Watch Videos: Earn points by watching short promotional videos.
  • Submit Feedback: Help us improve Reedemo and earn points by providing your valuable feedback.

Tracking Your Progress

Visit the Earn page to view quests available to you and claim rewards.

Redeeming Points

Once you gather enough points, you can redeem them for great rewards on the Redeem page.
